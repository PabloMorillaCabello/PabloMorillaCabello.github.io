Publications

Conference Paper

Exploring the Synergies of Modularization, Interface Standardization, and Service-Orientation in Production System Simulation

M. Birtic, P. Morilla Cabello, Á. Muñoz, A. Syberfeldt

SPS 2024 · Advances in Transdisciplinary Engineering, Vol. 52, pp. 185–195 · DOI: 10.3233/ATDE240164

This paper investigates a combination of concepts that promote reconfigurability and flexibility in production systems. By integrating modularization, interface standardization, and a service-oriented architecture, the work explores whether these synergies can positively influence the simulation process for production systems that may be in constant flux and reconfiguration — supporting faster and easier creation and updates of digital models.
